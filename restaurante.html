La Barraca?

Que es esto? Making fresh Mediterranean tapas for you is our greatest passion. We were especially inspired by the Spanish tapa. ‚Äç

You can come to us for a tapa with your wine or a refreshing cocktail, but also for "La buena mesa" a richly filled table with good food. We do this in one of Maastricht's most beautiful and historic streets. Together with our chef and our international team we are on a daily quest for the right balance between Mediterranean cuisine and our burgundian Maastricht.

just a question

We've tried to answer some of your questions.

allergens

First of all, we have a fairly wide selection of vegetarian tapas in our restaurant. Allergies and dietary requirements can also be indicated in the online reservation. It is important, however, that in case of serious allergies, you look at the menu beforehand and of course indicate it to the waiters on this evening. We will do our best to serve our tapas in the most appropriate way.
