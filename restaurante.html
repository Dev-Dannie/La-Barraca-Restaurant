Frequently asked questions

just a question

We've tried to answer some of your questions.

allergens

First of all, we have a fairly wide selection of vegetarian tapas in our restaurant. Allergies and dietary requirements can also be indicated in the online reservation. It is important, however, that in case of serious allergies, you look at the menu beforehand and of course indicate it to the waiters on this evening. We will do our best to serve our tapas in the most appropriate way.

How to eat at la Barraca?

We have a selection of changing Mediterranean tapas on our menu that you can enjoy after work, after shopping, or as dinner with friends, with family or as a couple. The casual atmosphere that comes with eating tapas and drinking good wine ensures a cozy 'Mediterranean' experience together. Our approach is therefore, not everyone has his own plate with food (main course), but to order some different tapas and share so that everyone can taste as much as possible. We try to realize a full meal on the table with tapas (smaller dishes). Our tapas are sometimes larger than expected and are made for sharing. It is a good idea that if you are 3, 4, 5 people or more, you consult with the service that you adjust the tapas to the number of people you are at the table with..

How it works at La Barraca?

You are welcome to join us up to 7 people. Up to this number of pers. we can make one table for you. You can easily reserve a table online up to 5 people (from 6 pers. please call us). We recommend for group reservations the La Barraca Men√∫ Sorpresa. This can be customized to your needs. You can also change your reservation online and possibly cancel your table yourself. At La Barraca we work with shifts, you can choose these yourself and they will be clearly shown in our confirmation email. We ask for your cooperation. If you are with more than one person or if you want to stay longer at the table, we advise you to choose the last shift. Of course, we must adhere to the set closing time.

Are dogs allowed inside?

Due to limited space, no dogs are allowed with us.
